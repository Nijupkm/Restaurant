Our Story

Born from Passion,
Refined by Time

Founded by Chef Aurélien Voss in 2009, Maison Noir was born from a singular obsession — to create food that tells stories. Every dish is a conversation between the bold spices of Southeast Asia and the classical techniques of French gastronomy.

Our kitchen sources exclusively from farmers within 100 kilometers of Paris, ensuring that each plate carries the authentic terroir of its season. We believe food is not consumed; it is experienced.
